What is a Bengal?

A domesticated cat breed from hybrids of domstic cats and Asian leopart cats. Bengals have a wild appearance with a golden shimmer, spotted coats, rosettes, and marble.

Bengal Temperments

Bengals are energetic and require a lot of attention. A smart, playful breed, they can be trained to play fetch and walk on a leash. Bengals get into a lot of mischief.

Bengal Adoption

Bengal Kitties will cost you anywhere from $1200 to about $5000. A widely popular house cat with a friendly nature and super affectionate attitude.

History

The Bengal Kitty history can be traced back to 1965 when an female Asian Leopard cat was mated with a domestic male cat and produced a littler of Bengal Offspring.

The Negatives

Bengals have a lot of energy and require a lot of attention. If you neglect your Bengal they will become sad and anxious. Sometimes they will urinate around your living spaces.

Best Practices

You need to do a lot of research before you decide to adopt a Bengal Kitty. They are very mischievous and termpermental, not for every cat lover out there.
